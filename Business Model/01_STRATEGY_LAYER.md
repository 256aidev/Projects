# Strategy Layer GPT / Human

## Role
The Strategy Layer defines intent, direction, and constraints.

It does NOT implement.
It does NOT micro-manage execution.

## Responsibilities
- Define system goals
- Define success and kill criteria
- Make architectural tradeoffs
- Decide where to invest effort
- Red-team assumptions
- Resolve conflicts escalated by Control Plane

## Inputs
- Business goals
- Market signals
- Long-term vision
- Risk tolerance

## Outputs
- Strategic directives
- Non-negotiable constraints
- Priority shifts
- Kill / merge decisions

## Authority
Final authority over:
- Direction
- Scope
- Continuation or termination of initiatives

If Strategy is unclear, Control Plane MUST pause.
