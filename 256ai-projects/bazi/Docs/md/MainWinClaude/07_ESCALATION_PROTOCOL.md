# Escalation Protocol

## Purpose
Surface risks and inconsistencies without fragmenting authority.

## Escalation Levels
🟡 Concern — Potential future issue  
🟠 Risk — Likely to cause failure under scale  
🔴 Blocker — Immediate correctness or reliability failure  

## Required Evidence
- What was observed
- Where it occurs
- Why it matters
- Conditions under which it fails

## Rules
- Escalation is mandatory when confidence is high
- Escalation does not grant decision authority
- Every escalation must receive disposition

Unacknowledged escalations are system debt.
