Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 25, 2026

256 AI ("we," "our," or "us") operates mobile applications available on the Apple App Store. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our apps.

Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials (such as an Apple ID or Google account token).

Profile Data. Information you provide within the app, such as preferences, settings, and profile details used to personalize your experience.

Usage Data. We automatically collect information about how you interact with our apps, including features used, session duration, and app performance metrics. This data is collected in aggregate and is not linked to your identity.

Device Information. We may collect device type, operating system version, and language settings to optimize your experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize our services
  • To process your account and manage your subscription
  • To generate personalized content and insights within the app
  • To improve our apps and develop new features
  • To respond to support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

AI-Generated Content

Some of our apps use artificial intelligence to generate personalized content. The data you provide within the app (such as profile information) may be processed by AI models to create this content. This processing occurs on our servers, and your data is not shared with third-party AI providers for their own purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — for app distribution and in-app purchases
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management
  • Analytics services — to understand app usage in aggregate

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our apps (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, safety, or property

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by:

  • Using the "Delete Account" option in the app's Settings
  • Contacting us through our support page

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the effective date above.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please visit our support page.
